MazeDash does not require a user account or sign-in.

Core control:
- Swipe in any direction to move through the maze.

Important review notes:
- Story Mode includes one-time mechanic tutorials for new gimmicks.
- Daily Challenge may show a daily prompt on launch.
- The in-game Shop uses gameplay-earned coins only.
- There are no in-app purchases, no real-money purchases, no ads, and no login system.

Modes:
- Story Mode: structured level progression
- Time Challenge: endless timed challenge
- Daily Challenge: fixed daily maze with bot benchmark rewards

The app can be fully reviewed without any credentials.
