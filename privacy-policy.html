MazeDash

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

MazeDash does not require an account or login.

Data Collection

MazeDash stores most gameplay progress locally on device. If you choose to use the optional online Time Challenge leaderboard, MazeDash sends limited score data to the developer-operated leaderboard server.

MazeDash does not use:

  • account creation
  • third-party analytics
  • third-party tracking
  • social login

Optional Online Leaderboard

If you use the online leaderboard feature, MazeDash sends the following data to the developer's server:

  • the player name you choose for leaderboard display
  • the Time Challenge duration category (1 minute, 2 minutes, or 3 minutes)
  • your best maze count for that duration

This data is used only to store and display leaderboard rankings. MazeDash does not require the leaderboard feature to play the game.

Local Data Stored on Device

MazeDash stores gameplay-related data locally on the user's device to support the game experience. This may include:

  • level progress
  • best scores and times
  • unlocked cosmetics
  • equipped cosmetics
  • coins earned through gameplay
  • tutorial completion state
  • settings such as sound, music, and vibration preferences

This data stays on the device unless you choose to use the optional online leaderboard.

Tracking

MazeDash does not track users across apps or websites.

Advertising

MazeDash includes optional advertising placements through Google AdMob. These ads are used to provide rewarded coins and occasional interstitial ads between natural gameplay breaks.

Google AdMob may process device and advertising-related data to serve and measure ads. MazeDash does not sell personal data and does not use ads to unlock gameplay power or advantages.

Third-Party Services

MazeDash uses Google AdMob for in-app advertising and a developer-operated leaderboard backend for the optional online ranking feature. MazeDash does not include social login services.

Children's Privacy

MazeDash does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of MazeDash change in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
